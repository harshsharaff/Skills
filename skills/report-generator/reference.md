# Report Generator Reference

## Section guidance

- **Executive summary** — one paragraph; the reader should grasp the outcome
  without reading further.
- **Key findings** — bullet list; each bullet pairs a claim with evidence
  (a number, quote, or source).
- **Recommendations** — numbered; each is a concrete next action with an owner
  or area where possible.
- **Appendix** — optional raw data, methodology, links.

## Tone

- Neutral and factual. Avoid hedging ("might", "perhaps") unless quantifying
  uncertainty.
- Prefer numbers over adjectives ("3x slower" not "much slower").
